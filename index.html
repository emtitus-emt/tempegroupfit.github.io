Welcome to ASU Tempe Group Fitness!

ASU Tempe Group Fitness classes are accessible to all enrolled ASU students! All Group Wellness instructors are prepared and trained to help participants of all levels meet their health and fitness goals. All class levels can be modified to meet beginning, intermediate and advanced exercisers. If you have injuries or conditions, please talk with your instructor prior to class beginning. Check out our schedule below and sign up for a class today on Fusion! Registration for a class opens 48 hours before the class time.
